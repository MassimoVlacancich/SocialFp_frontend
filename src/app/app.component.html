What is SocialFP?

Social Financial Predictor is a financial tool that makes use of social networks information to discover correlations between stock price movements and human sentiment. Twitter posts and SeekingAlpha provide both generic and specific opinions towards a certain company.

Social Analysis has to be accompained by traditional techniques such as Technical and Fundamental analysis, instead of being considered on its own; the intent is to incorporate investors emotions together with their strategies in taking a considerate trading decision.

The gathered information is processed to generate a sentiment movement index, Facebook AI Prophet is used to predict the future sentiment trend, which depending on the correlation level can provide a strong prediction. The final report is computed and sent to the user in about 30 seconds.
